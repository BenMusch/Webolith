The Quackle interface at the moment consists of a 'bookmarklet'. Please drag the following link to your Bookmarks bar in your browser.

Quackle Interface Bookmarklet

To use, it is very simple. Go to a cross-tables annotated game, and whenever you want to ask for Quackle's opinion on a play, click the bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar. Quackle may take up to a minute or two for some positions. The results will be shown under the game. These are only visible to you; if you wish to share them, simply copy and paste them to the discussion thread below!
