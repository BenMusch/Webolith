Note: This site will go down for maintenance for about 5 minutes at 11 PM PST on some days (I'll try to put up a schedule in the future). At this time I will push new code/features to the website and will eventually have a log/news of what has been done. If you are in the middle of a saved list your information will not be lost, but you may experience unresponsiveness during that time.

Note: For users coming from the desktop versions of Aerolith -- these will be discontinued (taken offline) on or around July 1st of 2011. The code will still always be available, but I would like to transition people to this site. Version 0.9beta was used by a few people and also allowed users to save, load, etc lists. If it is essential for you to keep these lists please talk to me and I'll see what I can do. It's not easy migrating them over to this new architecture, but I will try if you really want these. Otherwise, please be sure to finish your lists by the migration date. Please register for Aerolith again on this website.

Tip: this site works on netbooks too! Press F11 to put your browser in full-screen mode, and again to return to regular mode.
