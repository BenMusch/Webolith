Aerolith is an easy to use word study program
-  for games such as boggle, scrabble, words with friends
-
simply, create an account and you're ready to begin

when you begin, you can choose the american or world english dictionary
-

there are 4 different play modes:
  - challenges of the day - which include lists of words sorted by word legnth, words with blanks
 - search by probabiliy - which includes words that commonly appear in games
 - pre-made lists that include words that are high scoring, everyday words, or words with many vowels or consonants
 - lastly, saved word lists - which are lists of words that you have previously played, or upload your own custom lists
